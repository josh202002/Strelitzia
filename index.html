Living In Paradise

The villas are surrounded by pristine gardens and are designed with great detail, not to forget with fabulous touches. The foliage and flora at Strelitzia are undamaged and maintained by our team daily. I think it's time to turn off your phone, grab a cup of coffee and sit on our wrap-around porches, if you ask me, that is what I call relaxing. Nature

Strelitzia Villas, located in Paradise Plantation, is a secluded getaway, untouched and unspoiled by tourism and development. Our plantation is a sanctuary for a vast diversity of wildlife. Magnificent, wild but harmless rabbits, wild chickens beckoning you in the eve hours of the day. Wild orchids and carnivorous pitcher plants flourish in tropical forests. Peacocks, owls, kingfishers, woodpeckers, and flowerpeckers, are just some of the many other species of birdlife. Beautiful butterflies, cicadas, bats, and fascinating insects abound.
