Cedar @ Chikmagalur

Experience luxury redefined in our expansive 2400 sq ft villa, designed for discerning guests seeking comfort and sophistication. Step into a grand common hall with soaring double-height ceilings, leading to a chic loft adorned with resplendent hardwood flooring, a mini bar, and a powder room, setting the tone for an exquisite stay.

Discover magnificently appointed bedrooms, each a haven of refinement. Embrace grandeur with hardwood king-size poster beds, accompanied by lavish bathrooms featuring indulgent jacuzzis and invigorating rain showers, promising moments of relaxation. The living area, meticulously adorned with tasteful design elements, offers a cozy space to unwind. The villa's allure extends outdoors to a picturesque wrap-around porch, a tranquil retreat enveloping the home, inviting guests to immerse in surrounding beauty while indulging in a cup of coffee.
