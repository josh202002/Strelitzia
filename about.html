Strelitzia

Luxury Stay at

Chikmagalur

With every carefully chosen element, from the whispering woods to the gleaming glass, we’ve poured our hearts into creating spaces that harmonize with nature’s rhythm. Our villas and domes are not mere structures; they are love letters to the art of living, designed to offer tranquility and a deep connection with the natural world.

Our vision at Cicada is driven by a desire to redefine luxury, to transform it into an experience that transcends the ordinary. Here, luxury is not just seen or felt; it’s lived and breathed. It’s in the warm embraces, the quiet moments of awe amidst nature, and the shared laughs under the vast, starlit sky.

Join us at Cicada, where every stay is an opportunity to be part of something larger, a family bound by love for nature and commitment to creating unforgettable moments. Here, in our little haven, every visit is not just a getaway but a return to what truly matters – love, connection, and the timeless beauty of the world around us..
